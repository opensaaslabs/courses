# Roles

## Author
Creates or updates courses, modules, lessons, labs, assessments, and documentation.

## Reviewer
Checks correctness, pedagogy, completeness, source attribution, and maintainability.

## Approver
Accepts artifacts for publication after required checks pass.

## Instructor
Delivers or facilitates learning experiences.

## Mentor
Supports learners during labs, projects, and portfolio development.

## Learner
Consumes learning content and submits evidence for assessment.

## Issuer
Issues badges, certifications, or credentials.

## Verifier
Checks whether a credential is valid, current, and evidence-backed.

## Maintainer
Owns lifecycle, updates, deprecations, and archival decisions.
