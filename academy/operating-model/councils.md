# Councils and Governance Bodies

## Curriculum Council
Owns course strategy, learning paths, sequencing, and learning outcomes.

## Technical Council
Validates technical correctness, tooling choices, labs, and implementation quality.

## Certification Board
Owns certification criteria, assessment standards, credential issuance, and revocation policy.

## Skills Council
Owns skill taxonomy, competency levels, mappings, and workforce alignment.

## Governance Board
Owns policy, lifecycle, quality gates, risk, licensing, and publication controls.

## Community Contributors
Propose improvements, report issues, contribute learning assets, and provide learner feedback.
