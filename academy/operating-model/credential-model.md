# Credential Model

## Definitions

### Badge
A small recognition of a specific capability, completion, or evidence-backed achievement.

### Certification
A formal validation of capability across required courses, skills, projects, and assessments.

### Credential
Issued proof that a learner earned a badge or certification.

## Credential Rule

No credential should be issued without accepted evidence.

## Evidence Chain

```text
Learning Activity -> Lab/Project/Assessment -> Evidence -> Review -> Badge/Certification -> Credential
```

## Future Compatibility

The credential model should support Open Badges, W3C Verifiable Credentials, and DID-based verification.
