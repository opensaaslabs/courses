# Quality Contract

The quality contract defines required quality analytics.

## Required Dimensions

- Metadata completeness
- Governance completeness
- Learning outcome coverage
- Skill mapping coverage
- Lab coverage
- Assessment coverage
- JSON-LD coverage

## Required Outputs

```text
exports/analytics/quality.json
exports/analytics/graph-validation.json
```

## Rule

Quality scores are generated signals. They do not replace human review or approval.
