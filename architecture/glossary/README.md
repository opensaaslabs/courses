# Glossary

## Artifact
A versioned, governed object in the academy specification.

## Course
A structured learning unit containing outcomes, modules, labs, assessments, skills, and governance metadata.

## Module
A reusable section of a course.

## Lesson
An atomic instructional unit.

## Lab
A hands-on learning activity.

## Assessment
A method used to validate knowledge, skill, or competency.

## Skill
A specific capability a learner can acquire or demonstrate.

## Competency
A broader capability area composed of skills and proficiency levels.

## Badge
Recognition of a specific achievement or capability.

## Certification
Formal validation of capability across required courses, skills, evidence, and assessments.

## Credential
Issued proof that a learner earned a badge or certification.

## Evidence
Reviewable proof supporting a claim, badge, certification, or credential.

## Issuer
An organization or authority that issues a credential.

## Holder
The person or entity that receives a credential.

## Verifier
A party that checks whether a credential is valid.

## AEP
Academy Enhancement Proposal.

## SIG
Special Interest Group.

## Conformance
Proof that an implementation satisfies the academy specification.
