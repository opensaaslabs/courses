# Learning Artifact Lifecycle

Lifecycle states: draft, review, published, deprecated, archived.

A learning artifact may move forward only when evidence, ownership, review, and quality gates are present.

## Rules

- Draft content is editable and not considered canonical.
- Review content requires owner and reviewer assignment.
- Published content must pass schema and quality checks.
- Deprecated content remains discoverable with migration guidance.
- Archived content is retained for provenance and auditability.
