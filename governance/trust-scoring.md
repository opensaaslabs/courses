# Trust Scoring

Trust score represents confidence that a learning artifact is accurate, current, useful, and governed.

## Inputs

- Schema validity
- Review completion
- Source attribution
- Content freshness
- Assessment quality
- Learner feedback
- Evidence quality
- Maintainer ownership

## Scale

- 0-39: Not trusted for publication
- 40-59: Draft confidence
- 60-79: Reviewable
- 80-100: Publishable

Trust scores must not be treated as permanent. They decay when content becomes stale or unsupported.
