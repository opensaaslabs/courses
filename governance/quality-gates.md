# Quality Gates

A course is publishable only when it has:

1. Valid metadata against the course schema.
2. Clear prerequisites and target audience.
3. Measurable learning outcomes.
4. At least one practical lab or project.
5. Assessment or rubric.
6. Skill mappings.
7. License and source attribution.
8. Governance metadata.

Quality gates optimize for accuracy, usefulness, maintainability, traceability, and learner outcomes.
